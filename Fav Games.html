Way's Favorite Video Games


Top 5 Video Games according to King Way

#5: Naruto Shippuden: Shinobi Striker 3 & 4

Because I grew up watching the anime, it was nice to have a game to relate to the story

Great for people the liked the anime series.


#4: Fable 3 & 4

This game has a deep story with deep charecter development

It's easy to get lost with so much going on in the game


#3: Grand Theft Auto / Red Dead Redemption (The series)

These games are so imersive, it is difficult to remember that you have a life sometimes.

Deep story, realism, interactive NPCs and more! Don't get lost in the cityscape.


#2: Age of Empires III & IV

I am a big fan of stratagy games. I've always loved playing Risk but always found it slow. THis game is a good medium for those that love stratagy games.

Thes game will make you think of how to conquer and maintain your empire at the same time. Warhammer III is a bit more detailed and shares this spot as well


#1: Battlefield BC 2 Battlefield 3 & 4

A good mix of realism and arcade. A series of fun multiplayer shooters that are always packed with action and surising moments. Objective based games that require wit and mindful movements

If you are looking for more realism but massive multiplayer lobbies, Squad is a great option as well. Insurgency Sandstorm is for hardcore realists that prefer infantry heavy games.


Conclusion

I like action and violence. I think this list shows that but I am a lover of variety.
